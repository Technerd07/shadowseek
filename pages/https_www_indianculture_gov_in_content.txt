<b><p>03 JUNE   G SHANKAR KURUP (1901 to 1978)<p></b>
<p><img src="/system/files/diduknowImages/Screenshot%202025-06-03%20095345.png" class="pull-left pl-2" /> <p>Govind Shankar Kurup, a famous Malayalam poet, was born on 03 June 1901, in present-day Kochi in Kerala. Right from his childhood, he showed an inclination to write poetry. His first collection of poems was published when he was only 17 years old. Popularly referred to as G, his repertoire of published work includes more than 40 original and translated compositions. He was the first to win India’s highest literary award, the Jnanpith Award in 1965, for his anthology, Odukuzhal (Bamboo Flute), published in 1950. This work was later translated into Hindi and called Bansuri. He was also the recipient of the Padma Bhushan in 1968. G Shankar Kurup passed away on 02 February 1978.</p></p>
